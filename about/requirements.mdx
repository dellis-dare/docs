  <Step title="macOS Requirements">
    <Check>Zappl requires macOS Ventura (macOS 13) or later to run.</Check>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Network Requirements">
    <Check>Zappl requires outbound network access to the following services using port `443` (HTTPS)</Check>
    `zappl.co`
    >Required for sending data to the Zappl portal and for resources such as App icons, updates to the Zappl agent, etc.
    `dare.directus.app`
    >This is the application database which is required by the Zappl agent.
    
    <Warning>Additionally, any software vendorâ€™s public download URLs must be accessible.</Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Mobile Device Management (MDM)">
    <Check>Zappl needs to be deployed and configured using a suitable MDM platform.</Check>
    >Zappl integrates with most leading MDM solutions on the market. To get the most out of Zappl, ensure your MDM has the ability to send commands and/or scripts to computers. _Jamf Pro_ and _Microsoft Endpoint Manager (Intune)_ are two examples of MDMs which support this functionality.
  </Step>
</Steps>
